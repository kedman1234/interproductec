Design

Increasingly complex technical systems have special requirements when it comes to visualization. An intuitive UI may be the difference between requiring a highly paid expert to set up and maintain your systems, or empowering your machine operators to keep your machines up and running. To this end, we employ an in-house team of UI/UX designers specialized separating complex functionality from features used on a daily basis to increase a worker’s confidence and success in operating your machines. Heavy use of icons, custom-made for your products lower potential language barriers.

Especially highly automated and autonomous systems pose new problems to UI/UX design: how do you get the user to pay attention when needed? Operating systems outside their technical specifications, or failing sensors or equipment may require your personal to react when it counts.

We at InterProducTec are experienced in designing control units, control stands, and internet portals for a large number of production systems that focus the human operator’s attention to where it is needed.

If existing tools fail, we also don’t shy away from tailoring new solutions for your unique requirements.
